### Method Goals


This method aims to retrieve a booking with its full details.


### Request Format


The booking can be retrieved by several methods, such as the locator of the booking, the email associated to it, passenger name/surname or other
information relative to the itinerary itself.



### Response Format

The result returns a list of Itineraries, Passengers, Segments and Booking Locators. It may return also the Payment Info of the booking and the
information the client had provided to the provider, such as direction, phone number, etc.

| 191 | Unknown error or Integration/Application error.|
| 210 | Payment card error.|
| 211 | Passenger error.|
| 212 | Delta price error.|
| 345 | Connection timeout.|
| 346 | Operation not implemented.|
| 400 | Missing commission.|
| 500 | Login error.|
| 501 | Expired session.|
| 502 | Logout error.|
| 666 | Partial issuance error.|
